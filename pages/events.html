Burning Man Festival

Place:Black Rock City, Gerlach, Nevada, USA

Dates: 27 August - 04 September

During the annual Burning Man festival, tens of thousands of attendees immerse themselves in Nevada’s Black Rock Desert in the last week of August to create a unique, sustainable community circling around the famed bonfire of a massive wooden effigy. What began as a group of friends celebrating the summer solstice has evolved into an enormous festival focusing on community, inclusion, and self-expression, among other principles. "Black Rock City" has become a popular experience for those looking appreciate the creativity of humanity in all its various forms.

San Pedro Country Music Festival

Place:Paseo Público de San Pedro

Dates: 23 September

There are not many ways to explain it and the best way to understand what it is all about is to be part of it. San Pedro, the tourist city par excellence of the north of Buenos Aires has among its particular attractions, the only country music festival in Argentina and the first to be established as such in South America. Since 2003 it has been informing and contacting the followers of this genre, the San Pedro Country Music Festival started timidly in 2003 with a weekend in which rain complicated its development. Nevertheless, 21 groups from Argentina, Chile and Brazil forged bonds that have not been broken since then.
